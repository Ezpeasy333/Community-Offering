I love the name Talia
I love that in Jewish culture you get named after someone
So I’m named after Toby
I love that my name has a Hebrew meaning
I love that it’s something given to you
A first gift from your parents
The first sign that they care and love you is giving you a name

So now, as I start to feel disconnected from my name it makes me sad
I love the gift they gave me but it isn’t exactly who I am
Without my name who the fuck am I
I’m lost without my name
Even feeling this disconnect feels like I am betraying my parents, my family and my history

That was me
But is it still
I think part of me wants it to be
and another part wants something that fits better
But what could that even be
How do you tell someone you are giving back this beautiful gift they gave you
I don’t want to give Talia back because its a part of me
But maybe not all of me

I want a change but I don’t want to give up what I know

I'm scared to let go of something thats such a big part of me
Maybe because if I do I have to face the fact that I’m pretty sure I’m more than non binary
More trans then maybe I’m ready to admit or want to be right now

JULY 19th 2025
