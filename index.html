Community Offering

In school I was taught,
there is always something more to do
Somewhere better to get
Taught to constantly strive to be at the top,
to be the “best”
No matter how good of a grade I got
or how much praise I received
I was never happy
There was always something more that needed perfecting
Now, in my practice
I sometimes feel like I’m back there, in the classroom,
being conditioned that nothing is good enough
I tell myself be the best, you could be better, this needs perfecting
I evaluate my progress, my concentration how long or good my sit was
Finally, I remember,
there is nothing that needs perfecting
I can simply practice
Exactly as I am

This is an offering of various creative projects I have taken part in this semester. I have worked on not needing everything I share to be perfect or the most beautiful peace of art. This has been a hard challenge and contrary to how I was taught in school. Taught that everything could be better. Taught that only the best art should be shared and that if your art wasn't museum level you weren't an artist. I have worked to unlearn this and begun to feel like I can create art simply because it's what I enjoy. So, that is what I have done. I have gone on various creative journeys this semester that have all culminated here.

EZRA
